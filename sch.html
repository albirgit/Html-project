Chief Minister’s Honnahaar (ہونہار) Scholarship



Assalamualaikum

Dear Colleagues,
Shukar Alhamdollilah!
It is shared with deep sense of gratitude to the Almighty Allah, our three-hundred and forty-four (344) students out of six-hundred and twenty-seven (627) newly admitted students from BS batch of Fall 2024 have been selected on merit for the Award of Chief Minister’s Honnahaar (ہونہار) Scholarship. That means, almost 55% students of our newly admitted batch will be studying on Scholarship, for four years of their undergraduate education. Please find attached the list of PUCIT students, Congratulations! The Chief Minister’s Honnahaar (ہونہار) scholarship award ceremony is scheduled to be held on Wednesday December 4, 2024 and Madam Maryam Nawaz Sharif, the Chief Minister of Punjab, will be the Chief Guest. We are thankful to the University management and Madam Chief Minister for offering these generous scholarships at undergraduate-level. Congratulations to all of you.
